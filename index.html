Transmission Log

LOG_ENTRY // ORIGIN

Hong Kong doesn't switch off, and neither does this project. Astronomical Incompetence is a Hong Kong-based producer building hard dance, distorted dubstep and raw electronica for rooms that want to lose control on purpose — razor-sharp synths, crushing basslines and glitch-torn sound design running on dystopian energy.

LOG_ENTRY // TRAJECTORY

Every drop is engineered. Every breakdown is deliberately chaotic. The name is both the joke and the warning: astronomical precision pointed straight at collapse. Tracks land like a supernova compressed into four minutes — built for maximum sonic destruction, one bone-rattling drop at a time.

LOG_ENTRY // GROUND STATION

Rooted in the neon-drenched streets of Hong Kong, the sound channels the city's relentless pulse into futuristic soundscapes of controlled aggression — dense, fast, and unapologetically loud.

LOG_ENTRY // SIGNAL

If you're ready to lose control in the most precise way possible, this is your signal. Everything past this point is glitch by design.
